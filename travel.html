Travels in the Fifty Nifty

In the past 3 years, I've traveled to 30 states (that I can remember). Only a third of this travel has been the normal stuff - e.g. friends' weddings, family ski vacations, etc. The rest is the result of an awesome side project at work; as a speaker for Google's Brand and Reptutation Marketing Team, I've gotten to conduct public outreach programs on behalf of the company in a number of smaller U.S. towns off-the-beaten-trail.
